Values

Values are ideas or concepts that we believe in and use to guide our behavior and boundaries. I think of values as seeds of intention that determine how we live our lives and what we choose to be accountable to.

Below are my “core values.” I use this term to indicate that while I may value many things, these are the four most important values that I hold. I also have a “practices” section. This is informed by the Bay Area Transformative Justice Collective. Often times my practices are more specific, but for this context I chose to name 4 actions/ action oriented ideas that inform how I act out my values.



Core Values:

Embodiment
Respect
Consent
Humility



Practices:

Boundaries
Presence
Gratitude
Agency + Choice
