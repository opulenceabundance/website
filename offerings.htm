Conflict Navigation



Research indicates that conflict is a necessary process in creating resilient bonds and that moving through a conflict is a key aspect of building trust. I invite the relational networks I collaborate with to embrace conflict as a generative process that can refine communication and build capacity. However, without care, conflict can deteriorate connection and become, disintegrate bonds, and drain capacity.

That’s why its important to act now and not to wait!

Through listening, skill building, education, and facilitated communication it is possible for communication to be a catalyst for growth, deeper understanding, and increased capacity

Whether you are in a couple, organization, or community I am here to support you in exploring new ways to navigate conflict so you can break out of stagnant patterns and experience a new future!

Embodied Crisis Response Training



The Embodied Crisis Response is a holistic model that provides:

  • An inclusive understanding of crisis

  • Tangible techniques to respond to high intensity situations

  • Practical ways to strengthen crisis response skills

The Embodied Crisis Response model asserts that an activated adult cannot de-escalate an activated adult. For this reason, the Embodied Crisis Responses model relies on the person providing the intervention to stay embodied and work with a network whenever intervening on a crisis. This aspect of crisis response is often overlooked, leading to interventions that can result in the crisis escalating and in first responders experiencing burnout. The Embodied Crisis Response model seeks to provide first responders with the tools to engage in crisis response without sacrificing their own sense of inner peace.

Opulence created the Embodied Crisis Response Model in response to working with frontline staff and coaching them through de-escalating clients while managing their own trauma. This training is meant to empower staff with tangible ways to respond and de-escalate crisis situations.
