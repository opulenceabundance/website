Approach


My approach is guided by three distinct skillsets:

  • My formal education as a social worker where I received evidence-based trainings in Trauma Informed Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Motivation Interviewing, and Problem Solving Therapy.

  • My community based training as a Transformative Justice Facilitator where I learned about accountability processes, values and practices, and having honest conversations.

  • My commitment to my meditation practice where I have learned processes related to energy work such as cord cutting, energetic resourcing, and energetic hygiene practices.

While these skill sets draw on distinct and different bodies of knowledge, they all serve to create a safe and compassionate container for transformation, learning, and healing.
